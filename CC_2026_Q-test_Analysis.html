2. Filters

Critical score scale

  1. 1: Dangerous or scary performance. The driver has run out of patience with it. The system does not work. For TSI: detected a non-existent speed limit sign, or incorrectly changed to a speed where the difference from the real limit is dangerous. Must be improved.
  2. 2: The driver feels uncomfortable or dissatisfied. The system does not work correctly, but it is not critical. For TSI: the displayed sign is incorrect, but the speed difference is not very dangerous. Customer complaints are possible.
  3. 3: Neutral. The system may perform as expected or incorrectly, but it is indifferent for the driver and drivability. Customer complaints are not expected.
  4. 4: The behavior may initially seem strange or odd, but once understood it is acceptable. The behavior is correct, but the performance was not as comfortable as expected. For TSI: it detected the wrong speed limit but corrected it quickly. Customer complaints are not expected.
  5. 5: Good performance as expected or above. Customer complaints are not expected.
