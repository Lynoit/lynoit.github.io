NOA Off-Ramp Lane Change Feasibility Simulator

This simulator estimates whether a NOA system can initiate and complete an automatic lane change into an off-ramp zone. It uses a simple timing and geometry budget and visualizes the ego vehicle moving from the highway lane to a clearly marked right-side off-ramp lane.
