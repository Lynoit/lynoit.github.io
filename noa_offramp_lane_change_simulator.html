NOA Off-Ramp Lane Change Feasibility Simulator

This simulator estimates whether a NOA system can initiate and complete an automatic lane change into an off-ramp zone. It uses a simple timing and geometry budget: ramp zone length, vehicle speed, lane-change time, trigger delay, guard margin, map/localization errors, ramp width, lateral control accuracy and curvature severity.
